About EBS

This service is intended for emergency situations where social media is inaccessible, blocked or under heavy surveillance.
The messages posted to the local feed will not be censored by the site's administrator - as long as they do not contain child pornography, spam or other abusive content.
Currently only the last 1000 messages are kept in the local database.
Messages are only visible for about 24 hours.

There is no registration, login, CAPTCHA or any other form of mechanism to prevent anyone from posting a message.
This is intentional to allow critical information to be posted as soon and easy as possible.

The site's administrator does not take any responsibility for the content of messages posted by users - especially not for the accuracy of any descripted events.

NO PERSONAL INFORMATION IS LOGGED!
